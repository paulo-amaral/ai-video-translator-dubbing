# Changelog

## v1.0.0 - Initial Public Release

Video Translator v1.0.0 is the first public release of Paulo Amaral's AI video translation and dubbing toolkit for macOS.

### Highlights

- AI video translation pipeline with Whisper transcription, multilingual translation, TTS dubbing, and FFmpeg video assembly.
- Natural dubbing workflow with Gemini, OpenAI, ElevenLabs, Edge TTS, and gTTS provider options.
- WebVTT support for subtitle timing, smoother dubbing, and soft or burned-in subtitle tracks.
- Background audio preservation with original-audio mixing and optional Demucs vocal separation.
- Guided terminal interface through `translate.sh`, including automatic video-folder discovery and setup validation.
- Safe public repository defaults with `.env.example`, `.gitignore`, MIT license, and generated media/model artifacts ignored.
